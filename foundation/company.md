# Cerby

## What Cerby is

Cerby is an identity security platform that automates access, permissions, and governance across every app still being managed manually.

## Category

Identity security — specifically the slice of identity that existing IAM, IGA, and PAM platforms can't reach. Cerby calls this "disconnected apps" and (for nonhuman identity) "agent identity management."

## The core problem

Most apps weren't built to work with enterprise identity programs. They don't speak SCIM, they don't federate cleanly to the IdP, and they don't expose the APIs an IGA platform needs to govern them. So IT and security teams fill the gap with tickets, spreadsheets, and manual processes — provisioning by hand, offboarding by Slack message, running access reviews in Excel.

That manual layer is where risk lives:
- Ex-employees keep access to apps the IdP doesn't see
- MFA isn't enforced everywhere it should be
- Credentials don't get rotated
- Audits become fire drills
- AI agents get shared logins with no governance

Automated offensive tools are now finding and exploiting these gaps in hours, not days. Partial coverage used to be a defensible trade-off. It isn't anymore.

## What Cerby does

Cerby extends the reach of your existing IAM, IGA, and PAM tools to every app in the environment, connected or not. It does this with deterministic automation built specifically for identity — not RPA scripts that break on UI changes, and not LLM agents that guess. Same action, every time.

Concretely, Cerby automates:
- Joiner / mover / leaver provisioning across every app
- Session termination (kill switch) across the full app stack
- Access reviews with audit-ready evidence
- MFA enrollment everywhere, not just on connected apps
- Credential and password rotation
- Agent identity management for AI deployments

## Who it's for

**Identity Automation play:** IT and security teams at companies running enterprise identity programs (Okta, Entra, SailPoint, CyberArk, etc.) who have a meaningful number of apps those platforms can't manage — and who are accountable for the gap.

**Social Media Security play:** Marketing leaders and enterprise social media teams managing large, distributed social account portfolios across agencies, freelancers, and regions — and IT/Security teams at those same companies who are accountable for brand and ad-spend risk from unmanaged social access.

## Elevator pitch

Cerby is an identity security platform that automates access, permissions, and governance across every app still being managed manually.

## Positioning relative to the existing stack

Cerby does not replace IAM, IGA, or PAM. It extends them. No rip and replace. Cerby picks up the apps and identities those tools weren't built to handle, so the customer reaches 100% coverage without rebuilding what they already have.
